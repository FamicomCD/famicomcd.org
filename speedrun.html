Speedrun Competition

Get ready to take off running with the SMBCD Speedrun Competition!

The SMBCD Speedrun Competition Demo is two things in one! It's a demo for those who just want to try out the game, as well as a competition for those who want to speedrun the levels for a chance to become playtesters for development builds, or simply just want to speedrun the game! (Also just a note, the game will not receive patches until the Speedrun Competition ends to keep tatics and runs valid for the duration of the competition).

The game has a built-in timer, so you don't need to worry about your own timers! The competition is split into three categories due to different control schemes!: Hardware, Emulator using Keyboard, and Emulator using Controller/Touch Screen!

Join our Discord to submit runs!
