FamicomCD and our Branding

FamicomCD is a group that specializes in creating non-profit video games and video game modifications/hacks. The name relates both to the team, and the fictional Nintendo Famicom add-on that our games are based on.

Referring to the FamicomCD Team is different from referring to the teams of our projects such as The Super Mario Bros. CD Team (SMBCD Team) or TOASTERSOFT. Similarly, referring to our mascot and the group is different. Our mascot is named Discette or Disksan; Discette is usually used more by FamicomCD.
